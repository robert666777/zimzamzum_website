Frequently asked questions

How do I write a good prompt?

Be precise. Instead of "do my homework," try: "Log into my (Chaoxing, Yuketang, etc.) account, click on the homework section, open the homework called Homework 6, analyze it, complete it, and submit it." The more detail you give, the better the AI performs.

Which platforms does it work on?

Chaoxing, Yuketang and other Chinese educational platforms. You can also add your own platforms. Credentials are securely stored so you don't have to enter them every time.

Are my credentials secure?

Your credentials are stored locally on your machine. zimzamzum never shares them with third parties.

Can I create multiple accounts?

Yes, you can create multiple accounts. However, if you're on the Free plan, all accounts share the same credits.

What are the payment methods?

Alipay and WeChat Pay are the accepted payment methods.

Can I stop a running task?

Yes. You stay in full control — you can pause or cancel any task at any time.
